Question 4: What genres are most commonly produced and how do they vary between movies and TV shows?

In [10]:
from collections import Counter

def get_top_genres(df, content_type, n=10):
    genres = df[df['type'] == content_type]['listed_in'].str.split(', ')
    flat_list = [genre for sublist in genres for genre in sublist]
    return Counter(flat_list).most_common(n)

movie_genres = get_top_genres(df, 'Movie')
tv_genres = get_top_genres(df, 'TV Show')

movie_genres_df = pd.DataFrame(movie_genres, columns=['Genre', 'Count'])
tv_genres_df = pd.DataFrame(tv_genres, columns=['Genre', 'Count'])

fig, axes = plt.subplots(1, 2, figsize=(16, 6))
sns.barplot(data=movie_genres_df, y='Genre', x='Count', ax=axes[0])
axes[0].set_title('Top Genres in Movies')
sns.barplot(data=tv_genres_df, y='Genre', x='Count', ax=axes[1])
axes[1].set_title('Top Genres in TV Shows')
plt.tight_layout()
plt.show()

Both Movies and TV Shows focus heavily on International content, dramas, and comedies. TV shows have more content in Kids' Shows, Docuseries, and Reality TV, while movies lean toward Action & Adventure and Thrillers.

This shows how Netflix tailors genre offering by content type to suit viewer habits.

Conclusion¶

  • Steady Growth:
    The number of Netflix titles has grown steadily over the years, especially between 2016 and 2020. This shows that Netflix has been expanding its content library rapidly to meet growing demand.

  • Movies Lead:
    Movies make up a larger part of Netflix’s catalog compared to TV shows. This might be because movies are quicker to produce and attract a wide audience.

  • Global Reach:
    Most of the content comes from the United States, India, and the United Kingdom. Netflix is clearly focusing on global markets by working with different countries to create local content.

  • Audience Targeting:
    Many titles, especially TV shows, are rated for mature audiences like TV-MA and TV-14. At the same time, Netflix still provides family-friendly content, showing a strategy to reach viewers of all ages.

  • Genre Variety:
    TV shows often include genres like Kids’ Shows, Docuseries, and Reality TV, while movies are more focused on Action and Thrillers. This helps Netflix match different viewer interests depending on content type.

  • Suitable Runtime (Duration):
    Most movies are about 90 to 120 minutes. This makes the content easier to watch in a short time, which fits modern viewing habits.

  • Director Specialization:
    Some directors mostly work within certain genres. Netflix seems to continue working with them to keep offering high-quality content in specific areas.

Overall, Netflix is using a smart strategy to grow its platform. By offering a wide range of content in different formats and from many countries, Netflix can connect with many types of viewers around the world.
